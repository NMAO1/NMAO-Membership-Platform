What We Offer

Everything Your School Needs

Purpose-built for martial arts schools. No generic software, no compromises.

All-In-One Command Center

Members, classes, revenue, and tomorrow's schedule — your morning briefing on one screen.

  • Live KPIs: members, leads, sessions, revenue
  • Week-at-a-glance class calendar with enrollments
  • Today, yesterday, and month-to-date revenue
  • Membership alerts: paused, expiring, attendance gaps
  • Belt Progressions and Belt Tests built in
◆

Self-Serve Check-In Kiosk

Students check themselves in. Your front desk gets its time back.

  • Type-to-find with autocomplete from your roster
  • Family check-in for siblings — one tap each
  • Auto-deducts from session packs on check-in
  • Belt-color celebration screens for milestones
  • Runs on iPad, tablet, or any browser — no install

Lead Pipeline & Public Signup

A branded prospect-to-student funnel without the marketing-tech stack.

  • Public signup page on your school's URL
  • YouTube highlight reel, testimonial, and stats above the fold
  • Program-interest dropdown — leads arrive pre-segmented
  • Pipeline stages: New → Contacted → Trial → Converted
  • Follow-up automations included — no Mailchimp wiring
◈

Communications That Send

SMS, email, automations, and portal invites — one inbox, one outbox.

  • Two-way SMS thread per student, families merged
  • Broadcast templates: birthdays, promotions, renewals, belt tests
  • Triggered automations for expiring memberships and missed classes
  • Bulk magic-link portal invites for self-service
  • Audience filtering by program, status, and tag

Analytics & Reports

The numbers that actually move a martial arts school — not generic SaaS dashboards.

  • Lead funnel with conversion rates at every stage
  • Instructor comparison: classes, sessions, estimated payroll

  • One-click CSV export for taxes and accountants

Track ranks, attach video curriculum per belt, and run testing events from one place.

  • "Eligible to Promote" auto-detection by attendance and time
  • Belt test events with rosters and pass/fail tracking
  • Promotion history per student — never lose a record
