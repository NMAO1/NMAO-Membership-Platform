Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026  ·  Last Updated: April 10, 2026

National Martial Arts Organization ("NMAO," "we," "our," or "us") operates a martial arts school management platform including a member portal, staff application, and administrative dashboard (collectively, the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our Platform.

By using the Platform, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and date of birth provided during registration or enrollment.
  • Membership Information: Program enrollment, membership plan, billing dates, and payment history.
  • Attendance Records: Class check-ins, session history, and belt promotion history.
  • Profile Information: Profile photos, notes, and tags added by school administrators.
  • Authentication Data: Email addresses used for secure magic-link sign-in via Supabase Auth.
  • Usage Data: Basic interaction data within the Platform for operational purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Manage student enrollment, membership plans, and class attendance
  • Provide instructors and administrators access to scheduling and roster tools
  • Send secure sign-in links and account communications
  • Track belt progressions and promotion history
  • Generate reports and analytics for school administrators
  • Improve and maintain the Platform

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • School Administrators: Member and attendance data is accessible to the administrators and instructors of the school you are enrolled in.
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase for database and authentication services. Data is stored securely on their infrastructure.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, which employs industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Access to your data is controlled through Row Level Security (RLS) policies that restrict access based on your role and school affiliation.

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information, but no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong, unique email addresses for authentication.

5. Authentication

The Platform uses passwordless email authentication (magic links). When you request a sign-in link, your email address is used solely to deliver a secure, time-limited access link. We do not store passwords.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Platform is used to manage martial arts schools which may enroll minors. Student records for minors are created and managed by school administrators or parents/guardians. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children under 13 without parental consent provided through the school enrollment process.

If you believe a minor's information has been collected without appropriate consent, please contact us at the email below.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of certain communications

To exercise any of these rights, please contact your school administrator or reach out to us directly at the contact information below.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. School administrators may delete student records at any time. Upon request, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, except where retention is required by law.

9. Third-Party Services

Our Platform integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — Database, authentication, and file storage (Privacy Policy)
  • Google Fonts — Font delivery (Privacy Policy)

10. Cookies and Local Storage

The Platform uses browser local storage to remember your session and preferences (such as calendar view settings). We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
